LAST SEASONS TOP DRIVERS

John Davis

Our seasons top driver award goes to John, he drives a 2016 STI in the TRACK MODIFIED class and finished 1ST in his class and 3RD overall!

John Newgarden

John Newgarden made our list because of his numerous track records and podium finishes. He drives a Honda S2000 in the TRACK MODIFIED class and has finished 2ND this season and 5TH overall!

Jackie Ding

Jackie Ding is one of the fastest drivers in the UNLIMITED class. He came 1ST and 2ND overall this season. That's impressive, considering what cars are competing in the UNLIMITED class!

James Houghton

With several FWD class records driving his 2007 Honda Integra Type R, James Houghton is a driver to be afraid of! This season he finished 2ND and 2ND overall in the TRACK MODIFIED class!

Dalton Kellett

Dalton Kellett with his crazy modified EVO 8 finished 2ND in the UNLIMITED class and 6TH overall. He's a force to be reckoned on the track with!

Scott McLaughlin

Who said rotories aren't competetive? Scott McLaughlin proved those wrong! He finished 3RD in TRACK MODIFIED class and 7TH overall.

Conor Daly

Conor Daly with his 350z tore up the STEERT MODIFIED class, finishing 1ST and 3RD overall. Pretty impressive if you ask us!
